Wilbur Goodman

Only thirteen years old at the time of the 1900 Storm, Mr. Goodman shares his personal accounts of survival. He recalls how Galveston was a large, flourishing city prior to the storm.

There is nothing standing south of Avenue N. Everything is gone. Photo by W. A. Green | Taken at 19th and Avenue N.
